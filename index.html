HealthMate

Java based Hospital Management System

About HealthMate

The Hospital Management System project helps to solve common problems in managing patient and doctor information. Admins can easily log in to manage patient records, they can add, update, search, and discharge patients without hassle. This reduces paperwork and mistakes and improves patient care. Doctors can register and log in to see the patients assigned to them, with all the necessary information. They can also download their patient lists in .doc format, which helps them to stay organized. Overall, this system makes hospital management more efficient, improves communication, and enhances patient care. By simplifying administrative tasks, healthcare providers can focus more on taking care of patients.

Key Features

-The admin can login and control the management system.

By default the username and password is "admin"

-Admin can add new patient, update the patient information, search and discharge patients from the dashboard.

-Doctors can Register by filling the required informations.

-Doctors can login to their dashboard and can see the patients assigned to them.

-Doctors can download their patient list in .doc file.

More features are customizable.
